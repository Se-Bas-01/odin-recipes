Assasin's Spaghetti

Description

A wonderful dish of slightly chared spaghetti with a Rich, Smooth and Bold flavour

Ingredients

    For Broth

  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 3 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • 3 cups Passata
  • 3 cups Water
  • Salt to taste

    • For Pan

  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/4 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 200g Dried Spaghetti

Method

  1. In a pot, fry minced garlic in olive oil for around a minute or until golden brown.
  2. Then add passata, water and salt to the pot.
  3. Once at a simmer, turn heat to low.
  4. In a pan, heat olive oil then add chilli flakes and cook for a minute.
  5. Then add dried spaghetti and toss through the chilli oil.
  6. Once well tossed with tongs, pour 3 cups of broth over the spaghetti and stir every minute or so until slightly chared.
  7. Then pour 2 more cups of broth over the spaghetti and repeat this until is as chared as you like.
