Lasagna

Description

An Elegant dish of pasta, red sauce, white sauce, and CHEESE

Ingredients

    For Red Sauce

  • 3 cups Passata
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Italian Herbs
  • 1/2 tsp Massel Stock Powder

    • For White Sauce

  • 500gm Milk
  • 2 tbsp Melted Butter
  • 3 tbsp Flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • pinch of Black Pepper

    • For Base and Cheese

  • 1 cup Grated Mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup Grated Parmasan
  • 1 packet Dried Lasagna Sheets

Method

  1. Pour the passata and water into a pot.
  2. Once simmering, add salt, sugar, herbs and stock powder.
  3. Stir and leave on low heat.
  4. Add all white sauce ingredients in a pot on a low to medium heat
  5. Then cook sauce at high heat for 5 minutes.
  6. Next, put some lasagna sheets at the bottom of a tray then cover with red sauce.
  7. Repeat for 3 layers before adding 1/4 of the cheese in the middle.
  8. Then do to more layers then finish with some sheets white sauce and cheese.
